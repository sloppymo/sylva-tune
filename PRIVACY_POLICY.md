**Usage Information:**
- Login times and session data
- Feature usage and interaction patterns
- Support requests and communications
- Feedback and survey responses

### 1.2 Technical Information
We automatically collect technical information when you use our Service:

**Device Information:**
- IP address and location data

**Usage Analytics:**
- Pages visited and time spent

- Error logs and performance metrics

## 3. Data Processing and Storage

### 3.1 Data Processing
**Your Training Data:**
- Processed only for the purpose of fine-tuning your models
- Not used for training our own models without explicit consent
- Encrypted during processing and transmission
- Accessible only to authorized personnel for support purposes

**Model Outputs:**
- Generated based on your input data and configurations
- Stored temporarily for delivery and caching
- May be used for quality improvement (anonymized)
- Deleted according to our retention policies

### 3.2 Data Storage
**Location:**
- Primary storage in California, USA
- Backup storage in secure cloud facilities
- Processing may occur in multiple locations for performance

**Security Measures:**
- Encryption at rest and in transit (AES-256)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Compliance with industry standards

### 3.3 Data Retention
**Account Data:**
- Retained for the duration of your subscription
- Deleted 30 days after account termination
- Billing records retained for legal compliance (7 years)

**Training Data:**
- Retained for the duration of your subscription
- Deleted upon request or account termination
- Backup copies deleted within 90 days

**Usage Analytics:**
- Aggregated data retained indefinitely (anonymized)
- Individual usage data deleted after 2 years
- Logs retained for security purposes (1 year)
